ABOUT

ANTIMATADOR, an 8 man band from the South West UK, fuse cerebral vocals, tricksy horn licks, huge synths and earthy rhythms. They are a multi genre highly skilled outfit with NEW, ORIGINAL music spanning hip-hop, funk, jazz, electronica, soul, rock and DnB. SWUK based ANTIMATADOR deliver a must see live show which goes off like nuclear explosion of high energy, dancing and positive vibes.

The band launched in January 2014, formed from a group of professional session musicians with a long history of working together in high profile projects and with thousands of live shows under their belts. They wanted to expand their creative output and deliver an original sound through well-crafted tunes and the highest levels of musicianship. Raised on a rich diet of jazz, funk, soul, hip-hop and electronica from bands such as Rudimental, Snarky Puppy and Break Science, ANTIMATADOR are crafting a unique sound all their own that demands attention.
